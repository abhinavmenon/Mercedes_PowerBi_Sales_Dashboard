### Mercedes-Benz — Sales & FP&A Analytics (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze sales performance and financial KPIs for a Mercedes-Benz–inspired automotive dataset.

The dashboard is designed from an FP&A and management reporting perspective, enabling stakeholders to monitor revenue trends, regional performance, product mix, and key financial metrics.

## Business Objectives
- Track overall sales performance and revenue trends
- Analyze regional and country-level sales
- Identify top-performing models and segments
- Support FP&A-style decision making through KPIs and trend analysis

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query (ETL & data transformation)
- DAX (calculated measures & KPIs)
- Excel (data preparation)

## Key Metrics & KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Units Sold
- Average Selling Price (ASP)
- Year-over-Year (YoY) growth
- Regional contribution to total sales
- Model-wise sales performance

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive slicers (region, year, model)
- Time-series trend analysis
- Revenue and volume breakdowns
- Top-N product and market analysis
- Clean executive-style layout

## Key Insights
- Sales are concentrated in a small number of high-performing regions
- Premium models contribute disproportionately to total revenue
- Clear seasonality patterns in vehicle sales
- Growth opportunities identified in underpenetrated markets
